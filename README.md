# The Role of AI Chatbots in Enhancing Foreign Language Learning

## Project Overview
This research explores the impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and chatbots on modern language education. The project analyzes the capabilities of AI-based tools, their classification, and the opportunities they provide for personalized learning.

* [cite_start]**Classification of Chatbots:** Flow-based, AI-based, and Hybrid models[cite: 403, 406].
* [cite_start]**Pedagogical Impact:** Enhancing learner confidence and providing 24/7 practice opportunities[cite: 368, 369].
